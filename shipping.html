Shipping

We ship through a variety of freight services and sometimes personal delivery depending on the distance from Oregon. Rates are between $250 - $1000 depending on the company. We offer a $1000 discount on customer arranged shipping per shipment over 100lbs. If you are licensed, you can pick up hemp from us at our farm after vetting from the owners.
