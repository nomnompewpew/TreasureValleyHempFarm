About

Treasure Valley CBD and the parent company TVHF LLC are run by a group of individuals passionate about growing great hemp and providing it to the public as well as businesses that will help further the hemp and CBD movement. This site was made to help prevent re-sellers that misrepresent our products and offer a better price to businesses that will share applicable licensing information, Tax ID or EIN and sign a contract so taxes and regulations are appropriately handled.

We are seeking contracts to grow by the acre or pound for next year, please contact us if you are interested in dried hemp flower available as early as October 2019.
